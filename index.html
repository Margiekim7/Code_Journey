Motorcycles

A motorcycle is a two-wheeled motor vehicle that is steered by a handlebar and ridden on a saddle-style seat. Also commonly referred to as a "motorbike" or "bike," it's essentially a self-propelled version of a bicycle, with an engine providing the power.

Different types of Motorcycles

  1. Naked bikes
  2. Cruisers
  3. Sportbikes
  4. Offroad bikes

Common uses of motorcycles

  • Commuting
  • Leisure and racing
  • Offroad riding
  • Commercial and business
